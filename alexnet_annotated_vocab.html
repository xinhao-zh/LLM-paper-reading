Abstract 摘要

We trained a large, deep convolutional neural network to classify the 1.2 million high-resolution images in the ImageNet LSVRC-2010 contest into the 1000 different classes. On the test data, we achieved top-1 and top-5 error rates of 37.5% and 17.0% which is considerably相当大地，显著地 better than the previous state-of-the-art当前最先进水平.

The neural network, which has 60 million parameters and 650,000 neurons, consists of由…组成 five convolutional layers, some of which are followed by max-pooling layers, and three fully-connected layers with a final 1000-way softmax.

To make training faster, we used non-saturating非饱和的（不会梯度消失） neurons and a very efficient高效的 GPU implementation实现，实施 of the convolution operation. To reduce overfitting过拟合 in the fully-connected layers we employed采用，使用 a recently-developed regularization正则化（防止过拟合的技术） method called "dropout" that proved to be被证明是 very effective. We also entered a variant变体，变种 of this model in the ILSVRC-2012 competition and achieved a winning top-5 test error rate of 15.3%, compared to 26.2% achieved by the second-best entry参赛作品.

1 Introduction 引言

Current approaches to object recognition识别 make essential必不可少的 use of machine learning methods. To improve their performance, we can collect larger datasets, learn more powerful models, and use better techniques技术，方法 for preventing overfitting. Until recently, datasets of labeled images were relatively相对地 small — on the order of tens of thousands of images.

But objects in realistic真实的 settings exhibit表现出，展现 considerable相当大的 variability多样性，变异性, so to learn to recognize them it is necessary to use much larger training sets. And indeed, the shortcomings不足，缺点 of small image datasets have been widely recognized, but it has only recently become possible to collect labeled datasets with millions of images.

To learn about thousands of objects from millions of images, we need a model with a large learning capacity容量，能力. However, the immense巨大的 complexity复杂性 of the object recognition task means that this problem cannot be specified完全规定/定义 even by a dataset as large as ImageNet, so our model should also have lots of prior knowledge先验知识 to compensate for弥补 all the data we don't have. Convolutional neural networks (CNNs) constitute构成 one such class of models. Their capacity can be controlled by varying their depth and breadth宽度, and they also make strong and mostly correct assumptions假设 about the nature of images (namely, stationarity平稳性（统计特性不随位置变化） of statistics and locality局部性 of pixel dependencies依赖关系). Thus, compared to standard feedforward前馈（信息单向传递） neural networks with similarly-sized layers, CNNs have much fewer connections and parameters and so they are easier to train.

Despite the attractive有吸引力的 qualities of CNNs, and despite the relative相对的 efficiency of their local architecture, they have still been prohibitively贵到令人望而却步地 expensive to apply in large scale to high-resolution images. Luckily, current GPUs, paired with a highly-optimized implementation实现 of 2D convolution, are powerful enough to facilitate促进，使…成为可能 the training of interestingly（这里指）足够大的、有意义的-large CNNs.

The specific contributions贡献 of this paper are as follows: we trained one of the largest convolutional neural networks to date迄今为止 on the subsets子集 of ImageNet and achieved by far目前为止（最好的） the best results ever reported on these datasets. The size of our network made overfitting a significant problem, even with 1.2 million labeled training examples, so we used several effective techniques for preventing overfitting.

In the end, the network's size is limited mainly by the amount of memory available on current GPUs and by the amount of training time that we are willing to tolerate容忍，忍受. Our network takes between five and six days to train on two GTX 580 3GB GPUs. All of our experiments suggest that our results can be improved simply by waiting for faster GPUs and bigger datasets to become available.

2 The Dataset 数据集

ImageNet is a dataset of over 15 million labeled high-resolution images belonging to roughly大约 22,000 categories. The images were collected from the web and labeled by human labelers标注员 using Amazon's Mechanical Turk亚马逊众包标注平台 crowd-sourcing众包 tool.

ILSVRC uses a subset of ImageNet with roughly 1000 images in each of 1000 categories. In all, there are roughly 1.2 million training images, 50,000 validation images, and 150,000 testing images.

ImageNet consists of variable-resolution分辨率不一的 images, while our system requires a constant input dimensionality维度. Therefore, we down-sampled下采样（缩小） the images to a fixed resolution of 256 × 256. We did not pre-process预处理 the images in any other way, except for subtracting the mean activity均值（所有训练图像的平均像素值） over the training set from each pixel. So we trained our network on the (centered)中心化的（减去均值后的） raw RGB values of the pixels.

Q4 训练策略 数据预处理极其简单：缩放到256×256 → 减去像素均值 → 完毕。刻意强调是在"原始像素"上学习——不需要人工设计特征。

3 The Architecture 网络架构

3.1 ReLU Nonlinearity非线性（激活函数）

The standard way to model a neuron's output f as a function of its input x is with f(x) = tanh(x) or f(x) = (1 + e−x)−1. In terms of training time with gradient descent梯度下降（优化算法）, these saturating饱和的（输出趋于平坦，梯度接近零） nonlinearities非线性函数 are much slower than the non-saturating非饱和的 nonlinearity f(x) = max(0, x). Following Nair and Hinton, we refer to neurons with this nonlinearity as Rectified Linear Units修正线性单元 (ReLUs).

Deep convolutional neural networks with ReLUs train several times faster than their equivalents对应版本，等价物 with tanh units. This is demonstrated证明，展示 in Figure 1. This plot shows that we would not have been able to experiment with such large neural networks if we had used traditional saturating neuron models.

Q1 核心创新 第一个关键创新——ReLU
问题：tanh/sigmoid会"饱和"（输出接近±1时梯度趋近于0），导致深层网络训练极慢。
方案：f(x)=max(0,x)——输入为正时梯度恒为1，永不饱和。
效果：训练速度快6倍。
反事实论证："如果用传统激活函数，这么大的网络根本训练不出来。"

3.2 Training on Multiple GPUs

A single GTX 580 GPU has only 3GB of memory, which limits the maximum size of the networks that can be trained on it. It turns out that 1.2 million training examples are enough to train networks which are too big to fit on one GPU. Therefore we spread分散，分布 the net across two GPUs. The parallelization并行化 scheme方案 that we employ essentially本质上 puts half of the kernels on each GPU, with one additional trick: the GPUs communicate only in certain layers.

This scheme reduces our top-1 and top-5 error rates by 1.7% and 1.2%, respectively分别地, as compared with a net with half as many kernels trained on one GPU.

Q2 架构设计 模型并行：显存不够→把模型切成两半，每个GPU放一半卷积核。只在特定层（第3层）跨GPU通信。
意外发现：两个GPU自动学会了分工——一个学颜色，一个学纹理。

3.3 Local Response Normalization

ReLUs have the desirable令人满意的 property that they do not require input normalization to prevent them from saturating. However, we still find that the following local normalization scheme aids有助于 generalization泛化（在未见过的数据上表现好）. This sort of response normalization implements实现 a form of lateral inhibition侧抑制（相邻神经元互相抑制） inspired by the type found in real neurons.

Response normalization reduces our top-1 and top-5 error rates by 1.4% and 1.2%, respectively.

Q2 架构设计 LRN（局部响应归一化）：模仿生物神经元的"侧抑制"——相邻通道互相竞争。降低1.4%错误率。后来被Batch Normalization取代。

3.4 Overlapping重叠的 Pooling

Pooling layers in CNNs summarize汇总，概括 the outputs of neighboring邻近的 groups of neurons. Traditionally传统上, the neighborhoods邻域 summarized by adjacent相邻的 pooling units do not overlap. To be more precise精确地, a pooling layer can be thought of as consisting of a grid of pooling units spaced s pixels apart, each summarizing a neighborhood of size z × z. If we set s < z, we obtain overlapping pooling. This scheme reduces the top-1 and top-5 error rates by 0.4% and 0.3%, respectively.

Q2 架构设计 重叠池化：步幅s=2，窗口z=3 → 池化窗口之间有重叠。改进不大（0.4%），但有轻微防过拟合效果。

3.5 Overall Architecture

The net contains eight layers with weights; the first five are convolutional and the remaining three are fully-connected. The output of the last fully-connected layer is fed to a 1000-way softmax which produces a distribution分布 over the 1000 class labels. Our network maximizes最大化 the multinomial多项式的 logistic regression逻辑回归 objective目标函数.

The first convolutional layer filters the 224×224×3 input image with 96 kernels of size 11×11×3 with a stride步幅 of 4 pixels. The fully-connected layers have 4096 neurons each.

Q2 架构设计 完整架构：
输入224×224×3 → Conv1(96核, 11×11, 步幅4) → Pool → Conv2(256核, 5×5) → Pool → Conv3(384核, 3×3) → Conv4(384核, 3×3) → Conv5(256核, 3×3) → Pool → FC4096 → FC4096 → Softmax1000
设计规律：卷积核越来越小（11→5→3），通道数越来越多——从粗到细提取特征。

4 Reducing Overfitting 减少过拟合

4.1 Data Augmentation数据增强（人为扩充训练数据）

The easiest and most common method to reduce overfitting on image data is to artificially人为地 enlarge扩大 the dataset using label-preserving保持标签不变的 transformations变换. We employ two distinct不同的 forms of data augmentation.

The first form consists of generating image translations and horizontal reflections水平翻转. We do this by extracting random 224 × 224 patches图像块 from the 256×256 images. This increases the size of our training set by a factor of 2048. At test time, the network makes a prediction by extracting five 224×224 patches and their horizontal reflections (hence因此 ten patches in all), and averaging the predictions.

The second form consists of altering改变 the intensities强度（亮度值） of the RGB channels. We perform PCA on the set of RGB pixel values. This scheme approximately大约地 captures an important property of natural images, namely即，也就是说, that object identity身份（这里指类别） is invariant不变的（不受影响的） to changes in the intensity and color of the illumination光照. This scheme reduces the top-1 error rate by over 1%.

① 随机裁剪224×224 + 水平翻转 → 训练集扩大2048倍
② PCA颜色抖动 → 利用"物体类别不随光照变化"这一物理规律
测试时：10-crop取平均（4个角+中心 × 翻转）

4.2 Dropout

Combining组合 the predictions of many different models is a very successful way to reduce test errors, but it appears to be too expensive for big neural networks that already take several days to train. There is, however, a very efficient version of model combination组合 that only costs about a factor of two during training. The recently-introduced technique, called "dropout", consists of setting to zero the output of each hidden neuron with probability概率 0.5.

This technique reduces complex co-adaptations共适应（神经元之间过度依赖） of neurons, since a neuron cannot rely on依赖 the presence存在 of particular other neurons. It is, therefore, forced to learn more robust鲁棒的，稳健的 features that are useful in conjunction结合 with many different random subsets of the other neurons.

We use dropout in the first two fully-connected layers. Without dropout, our network exhibits表现出 substantial大量的，严重的 overfitting. Dropout roughly doubles the number of iterations迭代次数 required to converge收敛（达到稳定结果）.

Q3 防过拟合 Dropout——核心正则化技术：
原理：每次训练随机"杀死"50%的神经元 → 强迫每个神经元独立学习有用特征 → 相当于同时训练指数级数量的子网络
代价：收敛时间翻倍
位置：仅用于前两个全连接层

5 Details of Learning 训练细节

We trained our models using stochastic随机的 gradient descent with a batch size of 128, momentum动量（加速收敛的技术） of 0.9, and weight decay权重衰减（L2正则化） of 0.0005. We found that this small amount of weight decay was important for the model to learn. In other words, weight decay here is not merely仅仅 a regularizer正则化器: it reduces the model's training error.

We initialized the weights in each layer from a zero-mean Gaussian高斯（正态） distribution with standard deviation标准差 0.01. We initialized the neuron biases偏置 in the second, fourth, and fifth convolutional layers with the constant 1. This initialization accelerates加速 the early stages of learning by providing the ReLUs with positive inputs.

We used an equal learning rate for all layers, which we adjusted manually手动地 throughout training. The heuristic经验法则 which we followed was to divide the learning rate by 10 when the validation error rate stopped improving. The learning rate was initialized at 0.01 and reduced three times prior to termination终止. We trained the network for roughly 90 cycles through the training set, which took five to six days on two NVIDIA GTX 580 3GB GPUs.

Q4 训练策略 完整训练方案：
• SGD + 动量0.9 + 权重衰减0.0005
• 权重初始化：高斯分布(0, 0.01)
• 偏置初始化为1（让ReLU一开始就有正输入）
• 学习率从0.01开始，验证误差停滞时除以10，共衰减3次
• 训练90个epoch，耗时5-6天
关键发现：Weight decay不只是防过拟合，还降低训练误差——这一观察后来被深入研究。

6.1 Qualitative定性的 Evaluations

The network has learned a variety of frequency-频率 and orientation方向-selective kernels, as well as various colored blobs色块. Notice the specialization特化，专门化 exhibited表现出的 by the two GPUs: the kernels on GPU 1 are largely color-agnostic无关的（不敏感的）, while the kernels on GPU 2 are largely color-specific.

If two images produce feature activation激活 vectors with a small Euclidean欧几里得（距离度量） separation距离, we can say that the higher levels of the neural network consider them to be similar. The retrieved检索到的 training images are generally not close at the pixel level — the network has learned semantic语义的（含义层面的） similarity, not pixel similarity.

Q2 架构设计 可视化发现：两个GPU自发分工——GPU1学纹理/边缘（不关心颜色），GPU2学颜色特征。这种分工不依赖初始化，每次训练都会出现。
Q5 影响 深层含义：网络学到的是"语义相似性"而非"像素相似性"——一只站着的狗和一只趴着的狗在像素层面完全不同，但网络知道它们是同类。这预示了后来迁移学习的成功。

7 Discussion 讨论

Our results show that a large, deep convolutional neural network is capable of achieving record-breaking破纪录的 results on a highly challenging dataset using purely纯粹地 supervised有监督的 learning. It is notable值得注意的 that our network's performance degrades下降，退化 if a single convolutional layer is removed. For example, removing any of the middle layers results in a loss of about 2% for the top-1 performance. So the depth really is important for achieving our results.

To simplify our experiments, we did not use any unsupervised无监督的 pre-training预训练 even though we expect that it will help, especially if we obtain enough computational计算的 power to significantly显著地 increase the size of the network. Thus far, our results have improved as we have made our network larger and trained it longer but we still have many orders of magnitude数量级 to go in order to match the infero-temporal pathway下颞叶通路（大脑视觉处理区域） of the human visual system. Ultimately最终 we would like to use very large and deep convolutional nets on video sequences序列 where the temporal时间的 structure provides very helpful information.

  • 错误率近乎减半（§6）
  • 高层特征学到语义相似性（§6.1）
  • 三个前瞻预言全部应验（§7）

局限

未讨论可解释性、对抗样本、计算成本民主化；LRN和大卷积核非最优设计。

总结

一句话评价

AlexNet不是第一个CNN，也不是最优雅的CNN，但它第一次在工业级规模上证明了"深度+数据+GPU"的组合具有碾压性优势，开启了从"特征工程"到"端到端学习"的范式转移。
